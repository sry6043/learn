resp after = resp before + <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1">

This is used for mobile devices so that it doesnt zoom back(scale=1) and adapts to the device width.

or

Tells the browser to consider the width of the device as the real width of the screen and set its zoom level to 1, i.e., 100%, so it's not zoomed in or zoomed out.
