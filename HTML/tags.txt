<p>-paragraph tag.

<ul><li>-list
eg:	 <ul>
  	     <li>Coffee</li>
  	     <li>Tea</li>
  	     <li>Milk</li>
	</ul>

<ol>-ordered list(The list items will be marked with numbers by default).
eg:	 <ol>
  	     <li>Coffee</li>
 	     <li>Tea</li>
  	     <li>Milk</li>
	</ol>  

<span> - used to markup a part of text.
eg:  <p>My mother has <span style="color:blue">blue</span> eyes.</p> 

<div> - section in a document
eg: <div class="myDiv">
      <h2>This is a heading in a div element</h2>
      <p>This is some text in a div element.</p>
     </div>
